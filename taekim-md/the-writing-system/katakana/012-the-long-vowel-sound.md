#### 12 The Long Vowel Sound

Long vowels have been radically simplified in Katakana. Instead of having to muck around thinking about vowel sounds, all long vowel sounds are

denoted by a simple dash like so: ー.

Examples
- 1. ツアー (tsu-a) – tour
- 2. メール (me-ru) – email
- 3. ケーキ (ke-ki) – cake
- Summary
- All long vowel sounds in Katakana are denoted by a dash. For example, "cute" would be written in Katakana like so: 「キュート」.
