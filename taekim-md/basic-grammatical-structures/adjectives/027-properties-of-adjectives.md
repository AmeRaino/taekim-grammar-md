#### 27 Properties of Adjectives

Now that we can connect two nouns together in various ways using particles, we want to describe our nouns with adjectives. An adjective can

directly modify a noun that immediately follows it. It can also be connected in the same way we did with nouns using particles. All adjectives fall under

two categories: *な-adjectives* and *い-adjectives*.
