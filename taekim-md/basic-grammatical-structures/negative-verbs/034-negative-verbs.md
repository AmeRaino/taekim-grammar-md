#### 34 Negative Verbs

Now that we've seen how to declare things and perform actions with verbs, we want to be able to say the negative. In other words, we want to say

that such-and-such action was *not* performed. This is done by conjugating the verb to the negative form just like the state-of-being for

nouns and adjectives. However, the rules are a tad more complicated.
